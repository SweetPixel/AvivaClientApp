About Dentalink

dentalink is a provider network founded on the principal that quality private dental care, freedom of choice and reasonable cost can be achieved with the right mix of dentists, patients, and insurance. Dentalink is a preferred dentist network, not an insurance company. Our focus is to supply insurers, companies and intermediaries with access to a network of high-quality dental practices with a fixed rate fee scale.
